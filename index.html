About Me

Faiz Wahyu Setiawan

Hi, I’m Faiz Wahyu Setiawan. I am a Front-End Developer with a specialized focus on Color Grading and UI/UX Design. I believe a powerful digital experience lives at the intersection of high-performance code and precise visual storytelling.
My expertise lies in building seamless, responsive web interfaces while applying a cinematographer’s eye to every pixel. By integrating professional color grading into the digital realm, I ensure that every project isn't just functional, but emotionally immersive. Beyond the code, my UI/UX foundation allows me to design intuitive journeys that feel as good as they look. To me, a website is a canvas where technical logic meets cinematic art.
